🎯 Universal Process Intelligence & AI Opportunities Report

All Industries Comprehensive Live Data

Use this prompt for any company to get a comprehensive process intelligence report similar to the Mahindra & Mahindra analysis.

🔧 Variables to Customize:

  • [COMPANY NAME] - Target company name
  • [INDUSTRY] - Industry sector (Automotive, Manufacturing, Retail, etc.)
  • [SAP SYSTEM TYPE] - ECC 6.0, S/4HANA 2020, S/4HANA 2023, etc.
I need a comprehensive Process Intelligence & AI Opportunities Report for [COMPANY NAME] in the [INDUSTRY] sector. They are running [SAP SYSTEM TYPE - ECC/S/4HANA] and are interested in leveraging AI and automation to optimize their business processes. Please analyze their live SAP data across these key areas: 1. **Procure-to-Pay (P2P) Process Mining** — Extract cycle times, bottlenecks, approval delays by category/buyer/plant 2. **Order-to-Cash (O2C) Process Intelligence** — Sales order processing, delivery performance, billing efficiency, customer credit management 3. **AI-Powered Master Data Quality** — Detect inconsistencies in vendor/customer/material master data, suggest corrections 4. **Custom Code Rationalization** — Scan Z/Y objects, map to standard SAP functionality, provide upgrade advisory 5. **Financial Close Process Optimization** — Month-end bottlenecks, manual journal entries, reconciliation gaps 6. **Inventory & Warehouse Intelligence** — Stock anomalies, movement pattern analysis, slow-moving inventory detection Generate a professional HTML report that includes: - Executive summary positioning C16's capabilities against their process challenges - Live data extraction from their SAP system (actual numbers, not mock data) - AI/automation opportunities with implementation roadmap - ROI estimates and business case - Specific Fiori apps and SAP standard functionality recommendations - Custom development requirements where standard doesn't cover - 3-phase delivery model with timeline Focus on extracting REAL data from their connected SAP system to demonstrate C16's analytical power.
